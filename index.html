WHY PUBFOOD.js

Pubfood.js open source framework empowers publishers to take back control of their page performance and start maximizing revenue.

Simple

  • Seamless and straight forward integration
  • Simply copy and paste into existing framework
  • Implement non-blocking JS load control

Flexible

  • Easily integrate multiple bid partners
  • Configure header bidding partners to specific ad slots on configuration
  • House your data to your preferred platform

Transparent

  • Maximize yield through granular understanding of page and ad slot performance
  • Acquire bid analytics
  • Access latency analytics
